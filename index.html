OUR Story


Ana for Women is a great initiative that promotes the objective of changing the attitudes of people about menstruation and sanitary napkins. It focuses on imparting knowledge about menstrual hygiene and management among young girls in the remote areas of India. The project is headed by Ana and the project plans to take forward the personal hygiene for women, by conducting workshops and providing franchises.

WHY do we need awareness ?


In the remote areas of our country, talking about menstruation and women’s health is still considered as a taboo. Therefore, it is important that basic education about menstruation hygiene is imparted by mothers to their adolescent daughters. But due to the societal pressure, women do not talk about the issue.

The stigma around menstruation has caused several issues as it hurts the human rights of individuals. We need awareness as there are poor protections leading to severe infections. However, this issue arises due to the several practices that women go through as they cannot afford sanitary napkins and use a cloth that is extremely unhygienic.
