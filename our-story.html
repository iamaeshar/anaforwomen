OUR Story

Ana for Women is a great initiative that promotes the objective of changing the attitudes of people about menstruation and sanitary napkins. It focuses on imparting knowledge about menstrual hygiene and management among young girls in the remote areas of India. The project is headed by Ana and the project plans to take forward the personal hygiene for women, by conducting workshops and providing franchises.

Adolescence in girls is a very special period as it is a transition from girlhood to womanhood; however menstruation is an important sign of adolescence. Menstruation is defined as “the process in a woman of discharging blood and other material from the lining of the uterus at intervals of about one lunar month from puberty until the menopause, except during pregnancy.”

“52% of women worldwide are in reproductive age and most of them thus are menstruating monthly.

Still, the majority of them are not in conditions to take care of their menstruation in a hygienic manner or are impaired by it.”

But unfortunately, menstruation is supposed to be invisible and silent.


Mission and Vision

We at Ana for Women plan to provide adequate knowledge about sanitary napkins to women in the remote areas of the country. Our organisation Golden Wings provide durable and cheap pads for women who cannot afford the expensive ones and are still using cloth. We all know that there are several diseases that arise by using cloth, as it is not sanitised. Therefore, using Golden Wings pads, women can shift from cloth to sanitary napkins. We also encourage women who want to work by encouraging other women to use Golden Wings sanitary napkins.

The sanitary napkins made by Golden Wings are not expensive and are high quality; therefore women can purchase them without hesitation.

Taking care of yourself during menstruation is as important as taking care of your day to day activities. A little negligence in menstrual hygiene management can cause your body grievous harm and you may also act as an instrument for the spread of infection to others.
